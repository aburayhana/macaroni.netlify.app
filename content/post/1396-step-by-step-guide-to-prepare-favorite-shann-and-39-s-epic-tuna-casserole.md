##### Instructions to make Shann&#39;s Epic Tuna Casserole:

1. Combine all ingredients minus the pasta, Panko, Parmesan, and Fried onion straws.
1. Cook pasta slightly, about 5mins. Pasta should still be a bit crunchy.
