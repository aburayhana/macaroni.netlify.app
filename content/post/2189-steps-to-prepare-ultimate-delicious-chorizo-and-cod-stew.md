##### Instructions to make Delicious Chorizo and Cod Stew:

1. Heat the oil in a wok.
1. Add the onion and chorizo and fry both until the onions soften.
1. Add the can chopped tomatoes. Fill the can with water, shake it about, and add the water to the wok as well.
1. Bring the contents of the wok to a boil. Add the ketchup and stir well once the mixture is boiling.
1. Gently slip the cod fillets into the wok.
1. Reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for about 10 minutes.
1. Add the beans and let the mixture cook for another 3 minutes
1. Serve in bowls.
1. Enjoy!
