##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp Scampi:

1. Prepare 1/4 cup dry white wine
1. Get 1 tsp salt
1. Prepare 1 tsp pepper
1. Take 1 tsp red jalapeno in jar

1. Make ready 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
1. Take 1 lemons for side
